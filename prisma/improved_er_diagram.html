🔧 How This Addresses the Feedback

  • General Education Electives: `GeneralEducationRequirement` now supports credit constraints (9 credits from 63 courses) with min/max credits per course

  • Multiple Majors in Program: BBA program can have 6 different majors, each with unique core and elective requirements
  • Free Elective Blacklists: `FreeElectiveBlacklist` prevents specific courses from being used as free electives
  • Flexible Structure: Supports different plans, course offerings by semester, and complex academic policies
