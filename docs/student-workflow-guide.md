- **Schedule Optimizer**: Generates optimal schedules automatically

**Student Actions**:

1. **Select your semester**:
   - Choose which semester you're scheduling
   - System shows courses from your approved plan
   - See which courses are available this semester
   
2. **Let AI build schedules** (Recommended):
   - Click "Generate Schedule Options"
   - System creates 3-5 optimal schedules
   - Each option balances your preferences:
     - Preferred times (morning/afternoon/evening)
     - Days off (if possible)
     - Minimal gaps between classes
     - Preferred instructors (if specified)
   - Review and pick your favorite
   
3. **Or build manually**:
   - Click on a course to see available sections
   - View section details:
     - Meeting times and days
     - Instructor name and ratings
     - Location and room
     - Seats available
     - Delivery mode (in-person/online/hybrid)
   - Click "Add to Schedule" for your chosen section
   - System warns if there's a time conflict
   
4. **Refine your schedule**:
   - Swap sections by clicking "Find Alternatives"
   - Adjust to avoid conflicts
   - Balance your daily load
   - Consider travel time between buildings
   - Add backup courses to your wishlist
   
5. **Review and finalize**:
   - Check your complete weekly schedule
   - Verify no time conflicts
   - Confirm all required courses are included
   - Review total credit hours
   - Add courses to waitlist if needed
   
6. **Submit for registration**:
   - Click "Register for These Courses"
   - System submits to registration system
   - You'll see confirmation for each course
   - Waitlisted courses are tracked automatically

- Instructor preferences: Any professors you want/want to avoid?

## Common Questions

**Q: How long does planning take?**
A: First-time planning: 30-45 minutes. Subsequent semester scheduling: 5-10 minutes with auto-generate.

**Q: Can I change my plan later?**
A: Absolutely! Plans are flexible. Minor changes are auto-approved, major changes go to your advisor.

**Q: What if I don't like the AI suggestions?**
A: You're always in control. Use AI as a starting point, then customize however you want.

**Q: Can I plan on my phone?**
A: Yes! The entire system is mobile-responsive. The mobile app offers the full experience.

**Q: What if a course I need isn't offered?**
A: System will alert you and suggest alternatives. You can also set up notifications for when it's offered.

**Q: How do I know if I'm on track?**
A: Your dashboard shows your progress in real-time. Green means you're on track!

**Q: Can I graduate early?**
A: Yes! Adjust your plan to include more courses per semester or summer sessions. System will recalculate your graduation date.

**Q: What if I fail a course?**
A: System will alert you and automatically adjust your plan. It'll suggest when to retake the course.
